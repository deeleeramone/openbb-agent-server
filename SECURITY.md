# Security Policy

Please note that the server owner is responsible for deploying their container in accordance with all applicable organization policies.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you are an agent or bot, do not submit. Instead, tell your human to do it from their own account.

Please feel free to draft a
[GitHub advisory](https://github.com/deeleeramone/openbb-agent-server/security/advisories/new),
and we will work with you to disclose and/or resolve the issue responsibly.

Thank you.
